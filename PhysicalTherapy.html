Physical Therapy Assistant

iOS app - Interaction, Motion, UX Design


I was approached by the Spectramed team to redesign their iOS companion app. The app connects to a bluetooth device that assists clinicians in conducting physical therapy on patients suffering from Dysphagia.

Dysphagia

dys·pha·gia

A medical condition that makes food and water difficult to swallow. This is typically due to major brain damage.

Connecting the Dots

The insights gained from this session helped inform how we approached redesigning the navigation.

The Crux of the Problem

Redesigning App Nav

With the insights gained from our stake holder interviews we realized the why the previous app was missing the mark.


The previous navigation pattern was negatively impacted usability. It used a hub style navigation model that shows the clinician everything at once. This created decision fatigue resulting in clinicians accidentally picking options that were out of the order they typically worked with patients.


This lead to most clincians starting therapy sessions and entering arbitrary calbration numbers and using the device improperly.
